Labs across the country have had success in synthesizing meat from algae, fungi, and microorganisms found on plant roots. Our lab has partnered with Improbable Beef, a food startup to research candidate species. Our aim is to discover and document new bacteria. In particular, we are interested in bacterial species that have the ability to synthesize proteins that taste like beef. The human body is a source of thousands of types of bacteria, and different parts of the body harbor different species.
